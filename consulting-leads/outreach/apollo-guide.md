# Apollo.io Setup Guide

## Step 1: Create Free Account

1. Go to **https://www.apollo.io**
2. Click "Sign Up Free"
3. Use your personal email (not work)
4. Verify email

**Free plan gives you:**
- 100 email credits/month
- 10 export credits/month
- Unlimited searching

---

## Step 2: Search for Contacts

1. Click **"Search"** in the top nav
2. Click **"People"** tab

### Filters to Use:

**Job Titles** (add all of these):
- IT Manager
- IT Director
- Office Manager
- Operations Manager
- Technology Director
- Systems Administrator

**Location:**
- Austin, Texas (or Texas if you want more)

**Company Size:**
- 11-50 employees (small business)
- 51-200 employees (mid-size)

**Industries to Target:**
- Legal Services
- Financial Services
- Credit Unions
- Real Estate
- Manufacturing
- Accounting
- Architecture/Engineering firms

---

## Step 3: Export Contacts

1. Check the boxes next to contacts you want
2. Click "Save" or "Export"
3. Download as CSV

**Pro tip:** Look for contacts with verified emails (green checkmark)

---

## Step 4: Organize Your List

For each contact, note:
- Name
- Email
- Company
- Title
- Industry (so you can pick the right email template)

---

## Search Queries to Run

### Law Firms in Austin
- Job Title: "Office Manager" OR "IT Manager" OR "Administrator"
- Industry: Legal Services
- Location: Austin, TX
- Size: 11-100

### Credit Unions in Texas
- Job Title: "IT Director" OR "IT Manager" OR "VP Technology"
- Industry: Credit Unions / Banking
- Location: Texas
- Size: 50-500

### Manufacturing Companies
- Job Title: "IT Manager" OR "Plant IT" OR "Operations Manager"
- Industry: Manufacturing
- Location: Austin, TX
- Size: 50-200

### Real Estate / Property Management
- Job Title: "IT Manager" OR "Operations Director"
- Industry: Real Estate
- Location: Austin, TX
- Size: 20-200

---

## Daily Routine

1. Export 10-20 contacts
2. Send personalized emails using templates
3. Log in tracker spreadsheet
4. Follow up on 5-day-old emails
5. Repeat
